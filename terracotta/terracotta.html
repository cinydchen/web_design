about us

The Terra Cotta Coffeehouse is the perfect place to make your new, favorite, near-by coffeehouse. Located right in the middle of downtown Alfred, it is just a few minutes walk from anywhere in the town. We have several varieties of coffee, tea, chais, and a lot more. There is a deli menu, so you can enjoy sandwiches, focaccias, or wraps along with salads, pastries, and desserts. Our food is freshly made and taste delicious!
